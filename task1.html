MOIN & Welcome

NOTHING IS MORE RELAXING THAN HAVING NOTHING TO DO. HOLIDAYS AT THE NORTH SEA CAMP IS A VACATION BY THE SEA AND SIMPLY MORE RELAXATION

The North Sea camp in Norddeich, directly behind the sea embankment, is the gateway to the UNESCO World Heritage 'Wattenmeer'. The North Sea is right at your feet, the East Frisian Islands are within your reach and invite you for a visit. Fishing, cycling, inline skating, hiking, swimming, surfing, kiting, sailing... the range of leisure activities in Norddeich is great.

And if you feel like going for a trip to the city in between, that's no problem either - regardless of the tides, you can easily reach several shopping cities from here.

Important: You decide how you want to spend your holiday. Every single day!
